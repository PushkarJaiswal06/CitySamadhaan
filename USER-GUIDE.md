## 5. ğŸ“ CALL CENTER DASHBOARD

**Purpose:** Register complaints on behalf of citizens who call

### Tabs:

#### **File Complaint Tab:**

**Step 1:** Search Citizen
- Enter 10-digit phone number
- Search if citizen exists
- Or proceed with manual entry

**Step 2:** Fill Complaint Form
- Citizen Name & Phone (required)
- Complaint Title & Description (required)
- Department & Category selection (required)
- Priority (default: Medium)
- Full Address & Location details
- Ward number, pincode

**Step 3:** Submit
- Click "Register Complaint"
- Citizen gets complaint ID
- Complaint routed to department

#### **Recent Complaints Tab:**
- View all complaints you've registered
- Track their status
- See citizen details

**Call Center Agent Responsibilities:**
- Answer helpline calls
- Gather complaint details from citizens
- Register complaints accurately
- Provide complaint ID to citizens

## ğŸ”„ COMPLETE WORKFLOW EXAMPLE

### Scenario: Broken streetlight

1. **Citizen** files complaint via web:
   - "Streetlight not working near Park Road"
   - Selects "Electricity Department"
   - Uploads photo of dark street
   - Gets Complaint ID: CMP001234

2. **Department Officer** reviews:
   - Sees complaint in Electricity Dashboard
   - Assigns to Field Worker "Rajesh Kumar"

3. **Field Worker Rajesh** receives task:
   - Views complaint details and location
   - Clicks "Start Work"
   - Goes to location
   - Fixes the streetlight
   - Uploads "after" photo
   - Marks as "Resolved" with comment: "Bulb replaced"

4. **Citizen** gets notified:
   - Sees status updated to "Resolved"
   - Views field worker's photo
   - Rates complaint 5 stars
   - Adds feedback: "Quick response, thank you!"

5. **Admin** monitors:
   - Sees resolution time: 2 hours
   - SLA: Not breached
   - Updates city-wide metrics
